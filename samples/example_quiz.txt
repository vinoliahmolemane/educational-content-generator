Quiz: Photosynthesis (Beginner)

1. What is photosynthesis?
Answer: The process where plants convert light energy into chemical energy.

2. Which pigment absorbs sunlight?
Answer: Chlorophyll.

3. What gas is taken in during photosynthesis?
Answer: Carbon dioxide.

4. What are the products of photosynthesis?
Answer: Oxygen and glucose.

5. In which organelle does it occur?
Answer: Chloroplast.
